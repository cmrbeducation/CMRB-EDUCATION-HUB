## 2022: The Beginning

### Inception & Founding Vision

**Q1-Q2 2022: Foundation**
- CMRB EDUCATION was established with a clear mission: to democratize quality education
- Core team assembled with experienced educators and educational technology experts
- Identified market need for verified, accessible study materials
- Conducted comprehensive research on student learning challenges

**Q3-Q4 2022: Initial Launch**
- Launched pilot program with basic study resources
- First 100+ students enrolled
- Created foundational infrastructure for content management
- Established initial community of educators and content creators

### Key Milestones 2022
✓ Organization established
✓ Initial content library created (20+ subjects)
✓ First batch of students supported
✓ Community formation initiated
✓ Location established: Aairaj Nagar, Jodhpur
